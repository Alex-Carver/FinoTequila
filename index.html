OUR STORY

FINO Tequila is a 100% Women Owned brand. Created with passion, we provide an extremely smooth, premium, sipping tequilas that can be enjoyed by both men and women. The taste and flavor of our tequila is ensured from the outstanding quality, natural colors (without additives) and distinctive aromas from the finest agave azul, grown in the rich volcanic red soils in the highlands of Jalisco, Mexico. From place and production, to people and passion, Fino Tequilaâ€™s distillery has been a family business for more than three generations, defining mastery and commitment to quality of the product. The term FINO, in Spanish, means fine, smooth and suave. Our tequila represents exactly that - premium, easy to drink, smoothest tequila. We wanted to cater to a wider audience that typically do not enjoy tequila due to its burning taste. We guarantee...this tequila will be a love at first sip! FINO is also an acronym for Failure Is Not an Option. dates back to Apollo 13 mission manned by flight director Gene Kranz. We truly believe in this philosophy and this stems our exceptional dedication to our brand, FINO Tequila. Premium Tequilas

Blanco Tequila

FINO Tequila Blanco is made with 100% pure blue agave and created in small batches to deliver unparalleled smoothness and clarity, that is perfect on the rocks or in signature cocktails. It is unaged and bottled immediately after distillation to capture the bold flavors of the agave plant. A huge hit even amongst non-tequila drinkers due to its no-burn taste, FINO Tequila Blanco is easy to drink with rich sweet notes of agave and hints of vanilla, mint and cinnamon.

-NO SALT, NO LIME

Reposado Tequila

FINO Tequila Reposado is a small batch, 100% blue agave tequila, rested without overwhelming the distinctive aroma and flavor of agave in Jack Daniels whiskey barrels for 12 to 18 months (over 6 times the industry standard). Like fine jewelry making, our process behind creating FINO Tequila Reposado is astounding and takes time and expertise, making it impossible to mass produce.The result is a delicate texture with a smooth finish, that is perfect for sipping and great food pairings.

-INDEFINITELY NEAT


Reposado Recipes

Anejo Tequila

FINO Tequila Anejo is barrel aged in small batches for 3 years (3 times the industry standard) creating a perfect balance between the bold flavors of agave and charred oak. FINO Tequila Anejo is has NO artificial caramel coloring added to enhance the color (a very common practice in the industry) with rich sweet notes of agave and hints of chocolate, melon, vanilla and tobacco. It is best experienced neat like an aged single malt scotch.

-SUBTLE AND REFINED
